A tribute to my favorite artist.

About

For those unfamiliar with Odie, he is a 21-year old r&b artist from San Deigo. He is undoubtley one of my favorite artist in the game right now. Which speaks volumes to someone who's listened to 136,000 minutes of music last year - according to spotify. Everything about Odie is refreshing and exciting. His song writing, production and creativity are consistently immersive - giving you a roller coaster of emotions with each song. So, I highly reccomend you experience this for yourself, and give this king a listen.

I LOVE NEW MUSIC

Like most people, I got tired of listening to the same songs over and over again. I can't listen to frank ocean 24/7 - well you can but but theres so much good music out there to be discovered. Thus, I began my ventrue of finding new music, going to spotify to listen to a playlist called Mellow bars. 30 minutes into the playlist and Northface by Odie blessed my ears. It starts with a lo-fi aesthetic guitar and bass instruments that were aggresive but not intrusive. The production wasn't the reason I kept listening - ODIE's angelic vocals kept me from hitting next. His voice has a raw approach to it. A breath of fresh air in the world of auto-tune and heavy vocal processing. Furthermore, lyrically ODIE's storytelling of his experiences are creative and relatable. Telling through familiar themes such as hope, religion, ambition, goals, finding a soul mate, etc. Such things we've experienced or encountered at least once in our lives. Now, these themes can be found in every music but how ODIE presents them fills you with a different perspective. His lyrics aren't hallowed or valueless the words have an impact and stick to you like white on rice.
