About

For those unfamiliar with Odie, allow me to acquiant you. Spending most of his childhood in Toronto and now living in San Diego, the 21-year old artist has made me an absolute stan. His music is a breath of fresh air that draws inspiration from Kid Cudi and Coldplay. Additional inspirations derive from his family, hardships as an immigrant and Nigerian culture. Odies music advocates for self-improvement and enlightenment of self as he believes this is an important part of living. Using music as an outlet for expressing his feelings and thoughts – claiming in an interview that “I’m most alive when I’m able to express myself.”
